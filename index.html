About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! Well, a little about me. I grew up in small-town New Mexico graduating high school in 2008, college in 2012, and here I am today as an account manager at RetailMeNot! I have always wanted to be creative in my career and coding is the perfect way to be able to put that into my work. My interests range from sports to shoes to action figures to video games and I love music. I would also one day love to get into voice acting.
